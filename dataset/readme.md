##  LGG MRI Segmentation Dataset

The LGG MRI Segmentation Dataset contains brain MRI scans of patients diagnosed with Low Grade Glioma (LGG).  
The dataset is designed for medical image segmentation tasks, where the goal is to identify tumor regions from MRI images.  
MRI images are provided as grayscale brain scans showing anatomical structures and possible tumor regions.  
Each MRI image has a corresponding segmentation mask with the same filename for accurate image-mask pairing.  
The masks are binary images where black pixels represent background and white pixels represent tumor regions.  
The dataset is commonly used for training deep learning segmentation models such as U-Net and ResUNet.  
Preprocessing steps such as resizing, normalization, and augmentation are typically applied before training.
